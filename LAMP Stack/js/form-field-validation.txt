Form field validation for Group 9's project:

Regex for login/signup usernames:
/^[a-zA-z0-9]{3,32}$/

login/signup passwords:
/^([a-zA-Z0-9]|\!|\@|\#|\$|\%|\^|\&|\*){10,32}$/

regex for emails
/^\w+([\.-]?\w+)*@\w+([\.-]?\w+)*(\.\w{2,3})+$/
(from https://www.w3resource.com/javascript/form/email-validation.php)

regex for phone numbers
/^[0-9]{10}$/

first/last names
/^[a-zA-z]{1,50}$/
